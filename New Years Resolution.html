My New Years Resolution

As a young person with big dreams there are a couple of things I need to set right before I can welcome the blessings that are to enter my life. The first order of business:

  • I want to change my physical and mental health
  • For my physical health:
    I want to work on my body, internally and externally. I want to eat healthy and balanced meals but they don't need to be boring like rice, chicken and beans. I want to prioritize my gut health. I want to go back to attending the gym, I want to go to pilate classes once a week. I want to stay disciplined in my attendance at the gym and any and all classes I sign up for.
  • For my mental health:
    I want to work on expressing my emotions and not bottling things up. I've realised in the recent months that I tend to tuck things away in my mind and just try my best to forget about them. I've noticed that even though I hide them in deep and far away places in my mind they still seem to affect in some way or form. This year I want to start journeling what's happening in and around me, maybe even start going to therapy as well.
  • I am officially done with school, I am graduating in May 2025. I want to create a legacy or career I am proud of
  • I want to work in Tech, despite my BSc in Human Physiology
    My journey in tech actually started in 2021, in one of my first year modules at the time. The module was called WTW 155 Mathematical Modelling. In this class we were taught about Python, now at the time I did not realise that it would change my life for the better but here I am today with 4 certifications from SheCodes and many beautiful projects. Tech is a booming industry that has many opportunities, some may require experience and some may not. Either way this is the year I find my spot in tech. I want this job to be my early birthday gift to myself.
  • I want to see the world, but first I'll start with travelling in my own country
  • The world is my oyster
    At the end of this year I want to take myself to a different city in my country and see and try new things as a well done for surviving the first year of being an adult. I want to travel locally this december then 2 years after that I want to travel internationally, until I find a place that I would like to relocate to.
